Spleen Meridian Meditation

Beginning at the big toe and traveling up the inner foot, then to the inner leg and the groin area where it continues to the abdomen, internally to the stomach and spleen, moving further upwards to the throat and root of the tongue. Another branch travels down to the heart. 

As you meditate on this path, connect the inner legs, the stomach, up to the chest, throat and tongue. The taste is sweet, the color is yellow and imagine traveling through a marsh as  you pull your legs out of the marsh, lifting the energy upward.
