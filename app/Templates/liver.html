Liver Meridian Meditation

This meridian begins at the big toeand moves along the top of the foot and up the legsto enter the groin area then ascendsinternally to the liver and gallbladder as well as dispersing along the ribs and chest as it moves towards the neck. It continues to the faceas it reaches the eyes. A branch circles the cheeks and lips then moves to the top of the head.

As you meditate on the course of this meridian, imagine being under a tree and release what you don't need into the earth as you continue to nourish the body along the course of the meridian.
