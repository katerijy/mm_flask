Kidney Meridian Meditation

This meridian begins at the small toe, moving to the bottom of the foot then towards the ankle, traveling up the inner leg and enters the body at the base of the spine. It passes the kidney, urinary bladder then enters the abdomen, then the lungs, heart and pericardium. Another branch travels to the liverthen towards the base of the tongue.

As you meditate on this pathway, imagine being surrounded by water and sending nourishement to all the internal organs from the bottom of the foot up to the throat and back of the head.
